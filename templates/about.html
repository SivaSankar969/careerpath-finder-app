About Career AI

Empowering individuals to discover their ideal career path through the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning.

In today's rapidly evolving job market, choosing the right career path can be overwhelming. Our AI-powered platform analyzes your unique combination of skills, interests, and aspirations to provide you with tailored career recommendations based on real industry data.

How Our AI Works

1. Input Analysis

You provide your skills and interests, which our system processes using natural language processing techniques.

2. AI Processing

Our machine learning algorithm uses TF-IDF vectorization and cosine similarity to match your profile with career data.

3. Smart Recommendations

Receive personalized career suggestions with detailed information about salary, growth potential, and required skills.

Technology Stack

Backend Technologies
